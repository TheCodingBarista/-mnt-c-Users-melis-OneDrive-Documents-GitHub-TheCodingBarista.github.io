About

Hey there, I'm Mel! I have always been interested in people and interpersonal interaction. As such, my academic background includes studies in psychology and sociology, which helped me understand the intricacies of human behavior and social structures.

As a Full Stack Developer, I am skilled in Rails, SQL, JavaScript, and React. Coding has reinvigorated my curiosity and provided me with the stimulating challenge that I have been seeking for quite some time. I am committed to developing innovative solutions to complex problems.

Over the past decade, I have been a leader in both the retail industry and higher education, honing my multitasking, creative problem-solving, and strategic planning skills. I thrive in fast-paced environments that require quick thinking and adaptability.

With my unique combination of experience and skills, I aspire to work with socially-conscious organizations to make a meaningful impact on a larger scale. I am passionate about education and hope to leverage my expertise as a resource to help others.
